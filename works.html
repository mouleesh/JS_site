Development Projects

Online Marketplace Sales Management System

The product is developed in .Net and uses SQLServer as the backend and interface with leading online marketplaces such as Amazon and eBay. Through the Web Services API exposed by the marketplaces, it manages the inventories of sellers across marketplaces located in US, UK, Canada, Germany and France. Apart from Marketplace Sales Management, the product integrates with shipping providers such as USPS, Fedex and UPS. The product is consistently enhanced with features to cater the needs of its customers and to adhere to the change in functionality and API of the marketplaces.

Enterprise Application Integration Project

The scope of the project is to develop an Inventory Management System using the LAMP stack (Linux, Apache, MySql and PHP) and interface with legacy systems via ESB (Enterprise Service Bus). Inventory Management System will be interfacing with the POS system running on AS/400 to synchronize inventory and price. Also the Inventory Management system will be interfaced with CRM systems running on Windows and Linux.

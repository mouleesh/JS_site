Company profile

Jothi Software Private Limited is a software development company providing software application development and consulting services to clients in India and USA. Using agile software development methodology and industry best practices in distributed software development, we collaborate with clients to accomplish their goal on time and under the budget. With several years of On-Site consulting experience in the United States, we have insightful experience in all aspects of Software Development. This experience help us to understand our clients, their work culture and project requirements to build an extended team to suit their specific needs
We strongly believe that Quality Assurance is an integral part of the development process and the planning for quality should start right from requirements. We follow agile development methodology and actively participate in sprint planning and review meetings. Our technical project Manager acts as a liaison between the client and the team and play the role of a customer proxy to fill the gap with respect to requirements and play the role of the coach to help team members fulfilling the technical requirements. We provide technology solutions by means of product development, custom application software development, software maintenance and testing services. We are offering our services since 2011.
